At Ontario G1 Practice Test, we are confident in the quality of our study materials and want you to feel comfortable with your purchase. This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds may be requested.

1. 30-Day Pass Guarantee

If you do not pass your Ontario G1 Knowledge Test within 30 days of purchasing our subscription, we will refund 100% of your payment — no questions asked.

This guarantee applies as long as the refund request is submitted within 30 days from the original purchase date.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund under our 30-day pass guarantee, please contact us by email and include:

  • The email address used during purchase
  • The approximate purchase date
  • A brief note indicating that you did not pass the G1 test

Refund requests should be sent to: g1practicetestontario@gmail.com

3. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues accessing the Service after payment, please contact us as soon as possible. We will make every reasonable effort to resolve the issue promptly.

If a verified technical problem prevents access to the Service and cannot be resolved, a refund may be issued even outside the 30-day guarantee period, at our discretion.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may not be issued in the following situations:

  • Requests made more than 30 days after purchase (unless due to unresolved technical issues)
  • Failure to use the Service without a technical reason
  • Violation of our Terms of Use

5. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Approved refunds will be issued back to the original payment method whenever possible. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
