Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If you’re not satisfied, you may request a refund under the terms below.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you purchased a subscription and request a refund within 30 days of your initial payment, we will refund your payment upon request, unless prohibited by law.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at g1practicetestontario@gmail.com and include:

  • The email address used at checkout
  • The date of purchase (approximate is fine)
  • Any notes (optional)

Renewals / Cancellations

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled. You can cancel anytime to avoid future charges. Cancelling stops future billing but does not automatically refund past payments.

Payment Provider

Payments are processed by Stripe. Approved refunds are issued back to the original payment method. Timing depends on your bank/card issuer.
